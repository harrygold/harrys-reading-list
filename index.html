About Harry Gold

Harry is an eclectic reader. His library spans biography and memoir (especially music memoirs — Springsteen, Dylan, Bono, Flea, Chrissie Hynde), philosophy and spirituality (Ram Dass, Krishnamurti, Pema Chodron), business and tech (Naval Ravikant, Bill Gurley, Chris Dixon), classic and contemporary fiction (Garcia Marquez, Ishiguro, Philip K. Dick), history (Rome, Medici, Laurel Canyon), and art. He often discovers books through recommendations from people like Patrick Collison, Joe Rogan, Chamath Palihapitiya, Marc Andreessen, Rick Rubin, and friends. He reads across physical books, Kindle, and audiobooks.

A native Californian, Harry is a world traveler, an oil painter, a shark diver, and a soup maker.
